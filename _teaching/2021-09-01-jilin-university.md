From 2021 to 2025, I studied Communication Engineering at the School of Communication Engineering, Jilin University.

This stage provided my foundation in signal processing, communication systems, mathematical modeling, and engineering-oriented problem solving. These skills later became important for my work on thermal infrared hyperspectral imaging, radiative-transfer modeling, and physically grounded computational vision.
