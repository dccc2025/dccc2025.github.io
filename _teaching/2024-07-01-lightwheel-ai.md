From 2024 to 2025, I interned at Lightwheel AI, working on engineering tools for embodied AI workflows.

My work included embodied asset construction, RoboCasa plugin development, and teleoperation tool development. This experience strengthened my ability to connect model-side AI ideas with usable engineering systems, especially in simulation, data collection, and human-in-the-loop control workflows.

The internship also helped me build practical software habits around rapid prototyping, tool integration, and making research-oriented systems easier to use and deploy.
