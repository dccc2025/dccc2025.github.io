From December 2024 to February 2025, I interned at [Lightwheel AI](https://lightwheel.ai/), working on engineering tools for embodied AI workflows.

My work included embodied asset construction, [RoboCasa](https://github.com/ruziniuuuuu/robocasa) plugin development, and teleoperation tool development. This experience strengthened my ability to connect model-side AI ideas with usable engineering systems, especially in simulation, data collection, and human-in-the-loop control workflows.

The internship also helped me build practical software habits around rapid prototyping, tool integration, and making research-oriented systems easier to use and deploy.
