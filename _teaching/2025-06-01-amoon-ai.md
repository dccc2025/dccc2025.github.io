In 2025, I interned at Amoon AI, focusing on audio intelligence and deployable audio algorithms.

My work covered infant cry classification, fine-tuning audio foundation models, audio anomaly detection algorithm design, and edge-side deployment. The project required both model-level experimentation and deployment-aware engineering, including attention to robustness, latency, and practical runtime constraints.

This experience broadened my work from visual and embodied AI systems into audio perception, while reinforcing my interest in lightweight, physically deployable AI systems.
